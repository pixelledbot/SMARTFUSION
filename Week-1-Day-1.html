Week 1 - Day 1

Topics Covered

  • Meaning of SmartFusion

    SmartFusion is the integration of smart technologies like IoT, AI, and ML to build intelligent systems that sense, process, and act in real-time.

  • Introduction to IoT, ML and AI

    IoT connects physical devices to the internet.
    ML enables machines to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.
    AI is a broader concept of machines being able to carry out tasks in a way we consider smart.

  • Arduino and its Components

    Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. It consists of microcontrollers, input/output pins, voltage regulators, and USB interfaces.

  • Classification of Machine Learning

    Machine Learning is classified into:
    • Supervised Learning: Learns from labeled data.
    • Unsupervised Learning: Finds patterns in unlabeled data.
    • Reinforcement Learning: Learns via rewards and punishments based on actions.

  • Difference between IoT, ML and AI

    IoT is about connectivity and collecting data.
    ML processes and learns from that data.
    AI uses ML and other tools to simulate human-like decision making.
